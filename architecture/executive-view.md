# AiOpsVista AI Usage Collector Executive View

## Purpose

This architecture view summarizes the completed Case Study #004 AI Usage Collector platform and its role in the AiOpsVista delivery progression.

## Business Value

The platform now demonstrates a complete path from AI telemetry collection to centralized AI FinOps storage:

Provision -> Govern -> Observe -> Collect -> Attribute

This progression gives AiOpsVista a credible consulting story for enterprise customers that need AI cost visibility, governance, and operational accountability.

## What Was Delivered

- Terraform-managed Cloud Run collector
- Artifact Registry image pipeline
- Dedicated service account and least-privilege IAM
- BigQuery `ai_finops.ai_usage` integration
- Secure-by-default access control
- Evidence collection and teardown workflow

## Executive Outcome

Case Study #004 proves that the platform can collect telemetry, enforce governance, and preserve a path to future analytics and executive dashboards.
